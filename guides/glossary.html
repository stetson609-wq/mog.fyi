Mogging glossary (mog, mogged, moggle, looksmaxxing)

Working lexicon for readers encountering mog‑adjacent slang. Definitions are descriptive (how tokens get used), not prescriptive moral counsel.

Communities quarrel over nuance; treat entries as orientation guides, not courtroom exhibits. Where uncertain, sample live posts with archived links.

Core cluster

mog

Verb or noun stem—often "X mogs Y". Pairwise overshadow claim (longer definition).

mogging

Gerund naming the social activity or meme form—sometimes meta‑ironic.

mogged

Past tense frame: a person treated as on the losing side of a mog contrast.

mogger

Person positioned as doing the overshadowing—often celebratory or ribbing.

moggle

Platform‑adjacent or community portmanteau echoing pairing & spectacle (moggle & moghole).

moghole

Humorous metaphor for drop‑in comparison lobbies—see dedicated page.

Arena & live‑camera slang (informal)

Longer tactics: improve your Omoggle score.

mog maxing

Tongue‑in‑cheek label for dialing in lighting, camera height, distance, stability, and honest grooming so a live read lands at its best stable plateau—variance reduction, not punching down at rivals.

mog melting

When a capture collapses from motion blur, exposure hunting, bad tilt, or tilted posture—often after frustration—so scores swing ugly until setup resets.
